Imagine backend and frontend of a website is like a restaurant

Backend(logic, database) is the warehouse, kitchen

Frontend(user interface) is the hall and living room

API (application programming interface) is the waiter

When a customer order a pizza(buy product on website), the waiter (api) give the order for the kitchen(help to take the data from a remote server ), the kitchen take ingredient from warehouse(database), make the pizza(logic) and give the waiter to bring for you (display on screen)

An engirneer can do both called FULL STACK Engirneer
