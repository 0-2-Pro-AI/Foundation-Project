Focus on 20/80 work to reduce the working time

20/80 law is 20 percent this create 80 percent that

Example:

In economic, 20 % clients create 80% revenue (high-ticket product)
In biology, 20 % species create 80% amount (insects,bacteria,..)
In every skills, 20 % subskill create 80% ability to perform that skill ( for football is shoot, fake movement,...)
